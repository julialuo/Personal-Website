ABOUT ME

Hi! I am Julia Luo, a China-born and Canada-raised software engineer. I'm a rising junior at UC Berkeley studying Electrical Engineering and Computer Science, and I love both the engineering and research sides of computer science. I worked on the YouTube backend team last summer at Google, and recently joined the user security at Stripe for this summer. I am also an undergraduate researcher in the Robotic AI & Learning Lab under Sergey Levine exploring computer vision and robotics.

My less nerdy hobbies include soccer, which I have played competitively up until college, and snowboarding (rep'ing Canada). Also, I love both traditional and digital art, and can play the piano and bass clarinet.
